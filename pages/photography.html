What five things...

...do you need to get into photography?

DSLR Camera

The Pentax K-50 is the AK-47 of DSLR cameras — it’s inexpensive, consistent, durable, and perfect for beginners and more experienced users. The K-50 is feature-rich, but not too intimidating, and its build quality, as well as its picture and video quality, are excellent.

SD Card

You’ll need an SD card to store your photos and videos and to transfer them to your computer for editing. This 128 GB SD card is reliable and inexpensive, and it has write speeds of up to 100 MB/s, which means less time transferring photos and videos to your computer.

Tripod

In order to capture pretty landscapes, beautiful skies, or anything that requires long exposure, you’ll need a tripod to set up your shot and keep the camera still. This aluminum tripod expands to up to 61 inches and has well designed features like a built-in level, a handle to carry it by, and swiveling feet for use on uneven ground.

Camera Bag

This medium sized camera bag fits a DSLR body and two lenses, with room to spare for extra batteries, SD cards, a cleaning kit, cables, and whatever else you need when you’re taking your camera somewhere on the go.

Filter Kit

This filter kit comes with a UV filter, an FLD filter, and a CPL filter, all of which can be used to increase the quality of your photos under certain circumstances. It also includes a lens hood, which blocks out any bright lights just outside of frame, a microfiber cleaning cloth, and a snap-on lens cap and lens cap keeper.
