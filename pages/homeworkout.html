What five things...

...do you need to start working out at home?

Adjustable Dumbbells

With a range of 5 to 52.5 pounds each, this pair of adjustable dumbbells is the perfect place to start your home gym. They take the place of thirty individual dumbbells but only take the space of two. The SelectTech system lets you switch from varying weights with the simple turn of a dial.

Adjustable Bench

A solid bench can help you fully utilize a set of dumbbells, expanding your options to include bench press, incline bench curls, rows, and a whole host of other chest, back, and leg exercises. This one is sturdy and fully adjustable, and it comes with a 15-year warranty.

Pull-up Bar

This pull-up bar, which fits in most standard doorways, is ideal for chin-ups as well as wide, narrow, or neutral grip chin-ups. It can also be removed from the doorway, placed on the floor, and used for push-ups and dips.

Resistance Bands

These stackable resistance bands offer resistance from 2 to 75 pounds of resistance. The set comes with a door anchor, five bands of varying resistance, two detachable handles, an ankle strap, starter guide, and carrying bag.

Yoga Mat

Working out isn’t all about strength training. Stretching and balance are equally important. This 1/4″ thick yoga mat will give you the ideal place to warm up, stretch, or — I don’t know — practice yoga.

BONUS: Exercise Mat

If you need a soft mat to lay down before stacking any heavy equipment on your nice floor, or if you just need to cover a large area for stretching and warming up, these foam interlocking tiles will do the job. Each set of high density foam provides 24 square feet of high density fun.
