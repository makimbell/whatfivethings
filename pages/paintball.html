What five things...

...do you need to start playing paintball?

Beginner's Paintball Bundle

This bundle contains nearly everything you need to start playing paintball — paintball gun, protective mask, paintball loader, CO2 tank, six pods to hold your extra ammo, and a harness to hold your ammo pods and other gear.

Remote Line

Most paintball guns are made to have a CO2 (or compressed air) tank attach directly to them, which makes them heavy and unwieldy. With this remote line, you can keep the tank strapped to your hip or your back, and run the line to the paintball gun, making the gun much lighter and easier to aim.

Compressed Air Tank

Unlike CO2, compressed air doesn’t liquefy when compressed in a tank for paintball. This results in a more constant pressure in a wider range of temperatures, which means your shots will be more consistent. Whether you use a CO2 tank or a compressed air tank, remember to fill it with the correct gas.

Extra Paintballs

Most paintball facilities require you to buy and use their paintballs, which helps them keep gameplay consistent and fair. However, if you’re going to be playing in the backyard with friends, this set of 2000 biodegradable paintballs will keep you going for a while.

Tippmann 98 Custom

If you’d prefer to buy your gear individually rather than in a bundle, the Tippmann 98 Custom is a great place to start. This reliable paintball gun has an extremely wide array of upgrades designed for it, from stocks, to electronic feeders, to barrels, triggers, laser sights, and more.
